Optimizing Large Language Models

May 19, 2025 • 6 min read

Large Language Models (LLMs) have revolutionized AI, but their size and resource requirements present significant challenges. This article explores the latest optimization techniques making LLMs more accessible and efficient.

Quantization

Quantization reduces model size and improves inference speed while maintaining accuracy:

  • 8-bit quantization (INT8)
  • 4-bit quantization (INT4)
  • Mixed precision training
  • Dynamic quantization

Pruning

Pruning techniques remove redundant parameters:

  • Magnitude-based pruning
  • Gradient-based pruning
  • Layer-wise pruning
  • Structured pruning

Model Compression

Various compression techniques are being used:

  • Knowledge distillation
  • Parameter sharing
  • Low-rank approximation
  • Model pruning

Edge Deployment

Optimized LLMs are now running on edge devices:

  1. Mobile phones
  2. IoT devices
  3. Embedded systems
  4. Resource-constrained environments

These optimization techniques are making AI more accessible while maintaining high performance and accuracy. The future of LLMs lies in efficient deployment across various platforms and devices.
